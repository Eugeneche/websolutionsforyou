<h2>Project description</h2>
<p>

</p>
<ul>
<li>Easily add/remove service categories and individual services, meaning you don't need to contact a programmer for each change</li>
<li>After adding/removing a category or service, the changes are automatically displayed in the main menu</li>
<li>Feedback form (the service used allows you to send 200 free messages per month)</li>
<li>The names of services in the price list, prices, and special offers on the main page are saved and easily edited in an Excel file</li>
<li>SEO data such as "title" and "description" for categories and services are stored and easily edited in a text file</li>
</ul>
